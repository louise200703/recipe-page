Pancake recipe 🥞

A simple and delicious recipe, perfect as a snack. It can also be adapted into a sarvory meal for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 🥚 1 egg
  • 🧈 1 spoon of butter
  • 🥛 1 cup of milk
  • 🌾 1 cup of flour
  • 🧀 200 g of grated cheese
  • 🧂 A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Put all ingredients in the blender and mix all together.
  2. In a frying pan, pour small amounts of batter and spread it into a circle. Tip: add a little butter to the pan before pouring the batter so it doesn't stick.
  3. Wait until little bubbles form on the surface, then flip it.
  4. Repeat the process until all the batter is gone.
Ta-dah! Your pancakes are ready! If you want to, you can stack them and pour some honey on top. In my opinion, they are tastier this way!
